Founder

Khansa Khatoon

Creative Strategist & Founder of ALIFF

A full wardrobe.
Nothing to wear.

Every morning, I stood in front of a full wardrobe and still felt stuck. As a Muslim woman, getting dressed isn't just about style. It's about modesty, context, comfort, and identity, all at once.

Every tool I found ignored that. Fashion apps pushed trends that didn't fit my values. AI styling tools treated modesty as an afterthought. So I stopped looking for a solution and built one.

Aliff is the first letter of the Arabic alphabet. The beginning.

A tool that treats modesty as a first principle, turning your own wardrobe into thoughtful, wearable outfits without asking you to compromise who you are.

I'm a creative strategist and food photographer. I understand behavior and the small decisions that shape how we show up each day. I built this from lived experience, not a whiteboard.

✦ You shouldn't have to choose between modesty and style.

✦ You shouldn't have to overthink getting dressed.

✦ You shouldn't feel unseen by the tools meant to help you.
