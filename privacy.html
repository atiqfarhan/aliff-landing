Privacy Policy

Your Data is an Amanah

Our Commitments
01

Your Photos, Your Control

Everything you upload belongs to you. Photos are used only to generate outfit recommendations and nothing else.

02

No Face Photos Required

ALIFF does not ask for your face. We actively encourage you to keep your identity private when uploading wardrobe items.

03

No Selling. Ever.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your photos or personal data. Your wardrobe is not a product, it belongs to you alone.

04

Consent First

We only use your data with your clear, explicit permission. No hidden usage. No assumptions. No dark patterns.

05

No Marketing Use

Your photos will never appear in our ads, social media, or any public-facing material. We do not use your wardrobe to market anything.

06

Your Closet Stays Private

Your wardrobe is visible only to you. We do not share, display, or publish your clothing, with anyone, for any reason.

07

Delete Anytime

You can delete individual photos or your entire account at any time. Once deleted, data is permanently and irreversibly removed.

08

Minimal Data Only

We collect only what is strictly necessary to make ALIFF work. Nothing extra is gathered, stored, or retained beyond that purpose.
