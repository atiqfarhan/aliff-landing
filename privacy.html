Privacy is an Amanah

At ALIFF, your data is an amanah. We treat it with responsibility, restraint, and respect.

What We Believe

✦ Your photos belong to you
✦ Your closet is private
✦ Your consent is required
✦ Your data is not for sale

✦ Your Photos, Your Control

You own everything you upload.

We use your photos only to generate your outfit recommendations.

We do not take rights over your content.

✦ No Face Photos

ALIFF does not require your face.

We encourage you to keep your identity private.

✦ No Selling. Ever.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your photos or data.

Your wardrobe is not a product.

✦ Consent First

We only use your data with your clear permission.

No hidden usage. No assumptions.

✦ No Marketing Use

We do not use your photos for ads, marketing, or social media.

Nothing you upload will be posted publicly by us.

✦ Your Closet Stays Private

We do not share, publish, or display your wardrobe.

It is visible only to you.

✦ Delete Anytime

You can delete your photos or your entire data at any time.

Once deleted, your data is permanently removed.

✦ Minimal Data Only

We collect only what is needed to make ALIFF work.

Nothing extra.

In Simple Terms

Your photos are an amanah.

You own them. You control them. We protect them.
