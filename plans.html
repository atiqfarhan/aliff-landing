FAQ

How does the 7-day trial work?

Pick your billing cycle, enter your card, and start using ALIFF immediately. You won’t be charged for 7 days. Cancel anytime before Day 7 in your Apple or Google account settings and you’ll pay nothing.

What happens to my closet if I cancel?

Your closet and saved outfits stay accessible for as long as you’re subscribed. If you cancel, your data is preserved for 90 days. Resubscribe within 90 days and everything is right where you left it.

What does “founding member rate” mean?

Our first 500 annual subscribers get their price and limits locked permanently. Even when we raise prices or change plans later, founding members keep their original deal for as long as they stay subscribed.
